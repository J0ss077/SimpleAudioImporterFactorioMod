Simple Audio Importer — Mod Builder

Builds a ready-to-install Factorio mod from your audio files. No coding required.

Supported formats: .wav and .ogg only. Factorio does not support .mp3

Step 1 — Audio files

Drag & drop .wav / .ogg files here, or click to select
File Display name (shown in-game)

Step 2 — Mod info

You MUST select a Factorio version before generating your build.

Step 3 — Download

Where to place the .zip file:
Windows%APPDATA%\Factorio\mods
Linux ~/.factorio/mods
macOS ~/Library/Application Support/factorio/mods
Your sounds will appear in the Programmable Speaker under the instrument Simple Audio Importer.
